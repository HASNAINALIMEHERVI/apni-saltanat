Our Mission

Building a Marketplace
Based on Trust & Royalty

Apni Saltanat was founded with a simple vision: to create an online marketplace where Pakistani buyers and sellers can trade with confidence, fairness, and dignity.

  • Buyer Protection

    We ensure buyers can shop safely by vetting sellers and maintaining strict quality standards.

  • Seller Empowerment

    We provide Pakistani entrepreneurs a powerful, affordable platform to reach thousands of customers.

  • Halal & Legal Products Only

    Our platform strictly prohibits illegal or haram products. We promote ethical commerce.

  • Technology-Driven Growth

    Built with modern web technologies and Firebase, our platform is fast, reliable, and scalable.

Our Story

The Saltanat Story

Apni Saltanat was founded in Taxila, Punjab, Pakistan by Bablu Architect — a passionate developer and entrepreneur who saw a gap in the market for a trustworthy, locally-focused Pakistani online marketplace.

The idea was simple: give Pakistani sellers — small businesses, home-based entrepreneurs, and artisans — a platform where they could list and sell their products without the complexity and fees of large international e-commerce giants.

Built from the ground up with modern web technologies, Apni Saltanat has grown from a local idea into a marketplace serving buyers and sellers across Pakistan with the mission of making every deal feel royal.
