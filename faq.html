About Payments
Payments are made directly between the customer and the seller. Common methods: Easypaisa, JazzCash, bank transfer, or COD (Cash on Delivery). Sellers specify their preferred method in the listing or via WhatsApp. Apni Saltanat is not directly involved in the payment process.
1) Only buy from verified sellers. 2) Check ratings and profiles before making advance payments. 3) Report suspicious deals or excessive discounts. 4) Carefully review photos and details. COD (Cash on Delivery) is the safest method.
