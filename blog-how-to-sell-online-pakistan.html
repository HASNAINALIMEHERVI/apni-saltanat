How to Start Online Selling in Pakistan (2026 Guide)

Apni Saltanat Team April 4, 2026 10 min read

There has never been a better time to start an online business in Pakistan. This step-by-step guide takes you from zero to your first sale.

Step-by-Step Guide

💡

Step 1: Choose Your Niche

The ideal product has High Demand and reasonable margins. Top categories right now:

  • Fashion & Clothing (Pret, Abayas)
  • Mobile Accessories
  • Home Decor & Artisan Crafts
  • Herbal Beauty Products
✅ Start small with 5 high-quality products.
📸

Step 2: Quality Branding

In online shopping, your photos ARE your product. Use natural light and multiple angles to build trust.

🚚

Step 3: Reliable Logistics

Partner with TCS or Leopards. Fast delivery (2-4 days) is the key to repeat customers across the Saltanat.
