10 Smart Tips for Safe Online Shopping in Pakistan

Apni Saltanat Team April 5, 2026 8 min read

Online shopping in Pakistan is booming, but staying safe is essential. Learn 10 expert tips to shop securely, avoid scams, and get great deals in 2026. A complete guide for Pakistani online buyers.

The 10 Essential Rules

01

Always Buy from Trusted Marketplaces

The first rule of safe online shopping in Pakistan is to stick to verified, reputable platforms. Whether it's a large marketplace or a local one like Apni Saltanat, make sure the platform vets its sellers and has customer protection policies. Look for HTTPS, proper contact information, and clear terms of service.

✅ Pro Tip: Check if the website has a working contact method — if it's missing, be cautious!
02

Read Seller Reviews & Ratings Carefully

Before making any purchase, spend a few minutes reading seller reviews. Look for patterns — negative reviews about delivery delays or fake products are red flags. A seller with dozens of 4-5 star reviews is more reliable than one with only a few. On Apni Saltanat, the "Ameer" badge is awarded to top-rated sellers.

03

Use JazzCash or EasyPaisa for Secure Payments

While cash on delivery (COD) remains popular, digital payments through JazzCash or EasyPaisa offer additional records and protection. Always avoid sending money via direct bank transfer to an unknown seller before receiving your product.

⚠️ Common Scam Alert

If a price is 50–70% below market value, it's almost certainly a scam. Common tactics include sending empty packages or switching products. Always verify the seller's history.

04

Check Product Details Thoroughly

Genuine sellers invest time in quality photos and honest descriptions. Blurry images or suspiciously short descriptions are warning signs. Zooom in on photos and check the specifications.

05

Verify Seller Contact Info

A legitimate seller will have a WhatsApp number, email, or physical location. Send a quick message to confirm stock; their response time tells you a lot about their professionalism.
