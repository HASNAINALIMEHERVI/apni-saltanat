Mansa-e-Saltanat

The Imperial Manifesto

The Global Hub for Ethical Trade and Halal Earnings

Imperial Terminology

In this Empire, the Administrator is referred to as the Sultan. Sellers are honored as Sardaars, and buyers are recognized as Shehri (Citizens).

The title of Ameer is exclusively reserved for the Top 100 Sardaars (Sellers) based on their star ratings and customer reviews.

Ethical Boundaries

The ultimate goal of this Saltanat is to promote Halal income. Any form of nudity, obscenity, or the sale of prohibited goods is strictly forbidden and considered a grave violation. Ethical and faith-based trade is our foundation.

Fraud & Accountability

Any individual found engaging in fraudulent activities, scams, or failing to pay the maintenance fee on time will be Permanently Banished from the Saltanat without any prior notice.

Royal Treasury (Subscription)

To sustain the Empire, every Sardaar must contribute a monthly maintenance fee: 1000 PKR. This ensures the continuous operation and growth of our marketplace. No additional hidden charges will ever be demanded.

Reputation & Ranking

Your position in the Shahi Bazaar is determined by the citizens. Reviews and Star Ratings are the only criteria for reaching the rank of Ameer. Honest service leads to royal rewards.

Fee Instructions & Timeline

The monthly maintenance fee must be cleared on the 1st of every month. Sardaars must upload their payment proof (screenshot) directly through the Sardaar Panel to maintain their active status in the bazaar.

Vision & National Welfare

Our mission is to empower individuals through trade so that they achieve financial independence. We believe that Knowledge should be used for National Prosperity and welfare, rather than just being a tool for basic survival.

Muslim Business Ethics

Honesty and integrity in every transaction are mandatory. Prioritize the pleasure of Allah in all your dealings and strictly refrain from forbidden (Haram) wealth.

Responsibilities

Sardaars are responsible for maintaining the highest standards of customer service and product quality. They must ensure all transactions are conducted with honesty and integrity.The product should be safely packaged and delivered to the customer.The status of the product delievery(pending,confirmed,dispatched etc) should be explained to buyer through whatsapp or contact number.
