Imperial Rules

Saltanat ke Usool

Imperial Terminology

In this Empire, the Administrator is referred to as the Sultan. Sellers are honored as Sardaars, and buyers are recognized as Shehri (Citizens). The title of Ameer is exclusively reserved for the Top 100 Sardaars based on their star ratings and customer reviews.

👑 Account Status — Monthly Fee:
Marketplace ko maintain karne ke liye sirf 1,000 PKR per month ki fee rakhi gayi hai, jo tab lazim hogi jab aapka status manual check ke baad change kiya jayega.

Fees pay karne wale Sardaars ko: Unlimited products, verified badge, aur priority listing milti hai.

Ethical Boundaries

The ultimate goal of this Saltanat is to promote Halal income. Any form of nudity, obscenity, or the sale of prohibited goods is strictly forbidden and considered a grave violation. Ethical and faith-based trade is our foundation.

Fraud & Accountability

Any individual found engaging in fraudulent activities, scams, or failing to pay the maintenance fee on time will be Permanently Banished from the Saltanat without any prior notice. The Sultan's decision is final.

Reputation & Ranking

Your position in the Shahi Bazaar is determined by the citizens. Reviews and Star Ratings are the only criteria for reaching the rank of Ameer. Honest service leads to royal rewards.

Fee Instructions & Timeline

The monthly maintenance fee must be cleared on the 1st of every month. Sardaars must upload their payment proof (screenshot) directly through the Sardaar Panel to maintain their active status in the bazaar.

Seller Responsibilities

Sardaars are responsible for maintaining the highest standards of customer service and product quality. Products should be safely packaged and delivered. The status of delivery (pending, confirmed, dispatched, etc.) should be communicated to the buyer through WhatsApp or contact number.

Muslim Business Ethics

Honesty and integrity in every transaction are mandatory. Prioritize the pleasure of Allah in all your dealings and strictly refrain from forbidden (Haram) wealth. "Behtareen kamaai wo hai jo apne hathon ki mehnat aur halal tijarat se ho."

Vision & National Welfare

Our mission is to empower individuals through trade so that they achieve financial independence. We believe that Knowledge should be used for National Prosperity and welfare, rather than just being a tool for basic survival.
